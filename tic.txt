Events Page — Layout Structure
Yes, both future and past events should exist, but they are treated differently.

1. Page Header (Top)
Page title: Events
One-line description:
“Focused sessions designed for clarity and execution.”
This sets context before any listings.

2. Upcoming Events (Primary Focus)
This is the main section of the page.
Layout:
Vertical list or clean cards
Each event shows:
Event name
One-line purpose
Date
Format (Online / In-person)
Access level (Explorer / Visionary / Invite-only)
CTA (Register / Request Access)
Rules:
If there are no upcoming events, show:
“No upcoming events scheduled.”
3. Past Events (Secondary, De-emphasized)
Placed below upcoming events.
Layout:
Simple list (not cards)
Minimal information:
Event name
Month / Year
Format
Optional:
“View summary” (members only)
Rules:
No images
No highlights
No recordings linked publicly
Purpose:
Establish credibility without turning events into content.
4. Access Note (Bottom)
Short block explaining:
Events are limited
Access depends on membership and event type
Not all events are open to everyone
5. Footer
Standard site footer.

visionary : 

Based on the system code, the Visionary tier is the "Deep Execution" level of The Incite Crew. It is designed for founders who have moved past the initial ideation phase and are ready to professionalize their brand and scale their operations with high intent.

Priced at ₹14,999, it shifts the focus from simple structure to building a high-output growth engine.

## Core Offering: Brand & Growth System
The Visionary tier is built around "Deep execution support with focused involvement." Unlike the self-serve nature of the Explorer tier, this level provides direct access to systems that automate and professionalize a founder's output.

### Key Deliverables
Brand Architecture & Narrative: A complete overhaul of how the company presents itself to the market.

The Content Engine: Transitioning from "starter" content to a consistent messaging engine that drives growth.

Offer & Funnel Structuring: Strategic help in building the actual pathways that turn audiences into customers.

Design & R&D Power: * 4–6 Custom Design Assets per month (compared to only 1 for Explorers).

Full Monthly R&D Reports and Advanced Audience Intelligence.

### Exclusive Visionary Features
According to the OfferingsSection and Membership code, Visionaries receive several high-touch benefits that Explorers do not:

1:1 Strategy Sessions: Direct collaborative time to define roadmaps.

Priority Support: Direct access for faster reviews and decision-making support.

Custom Execution Roadmap: A tailored plan rather than just generic templates.

Quarterly Business Reviews: A high-level look at the year’s progress to ensure alignment.

## The "Visionary" Profile
In the WhoItsForSection, the Visionary tier aligns with the "Seekers" and "Executors"—leaders who have found a degree of traction but are currently overwhelmed by chaos. The goal of this tier is to provide the "Strategic Structure" needed to scale rapidly without "grinding harder.
